About

I'm a PhD student in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Trento. My research focuses on heterogeneous multi-teacher distillation and foundation models for satellite imagery (e.g., Sentinel-2), combining self-supervised learning, representation learning, and uncertainty-aware methods.

More broadly, I'm interested in how modern AI systems can become reliable decision-making tools: calibrated uncertainty, selective prediction, and hybrid human-AI workflows.

Research Interests

  • Foundation models for Earth Observation and remote sensing.
  • Multi-teacher and multi-modal knowledge distillation for satellite imagery.
  • Selective prediction, learning to defer, and uncertainty quantification.
  • Neural network interpretability and controllability of large models.
